### Suzuka, Japan

- **Date**: April 6, 2025
- **Winner**: Max VERSTAPPEN
- **Second Place**: Lando NORRIS

Max Verstappen returned to form with a win in Suzuka, a circuit where precision and focus are crucial, trailed by Lando Norris who continued his consistent performance.

### Sakhir, Bahrain

- **Date**: April 13, 2025
- **Winner**: Oscar PIASTRI
- **Second Place**: George RUSSELL

- **Lando Norris**: A strong contender across multiple races, securing a first place in Melbourne and consistently achieving podium finishes.

- **Oscar Piastri**: A breakout talent of the season, clinching victories in Shanghai and Sakhir, indicating his potential to be a future champion.
